Consultant Psychiatrist

Dr. Charuni Gamage

MBBS, MD (Psychiatry), FRANZCP

Dr. Charuni Gamage is a Consultant Psychiatrist at Bayview Specialist Clinic. With over 20 years of experience treating adults with a wide range of psychiatric conditions both domestically and internationally, she brings exceptional expertise and compassionate care to every patient.

Her approach integrates biological, psychological, and social perspectives in assessment, combining evidence-based pharmacological treatment with Cognitive Behaviour Therapy and other psychotherapies when appropriate. Dr. Gamage is committed to patient-centred care, ensuring each individual receives a tailored treatment plan.
